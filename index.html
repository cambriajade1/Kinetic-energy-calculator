üí° Key Concepts

Energy Conservation:
When an object falls, potential energy converts to kinetic energy. The total mechanical energy remains constant (ignoring air resistance).
Example:
A 1 kg ball dropped from 20 m:
Initial: PE = 196.2 J, KE = 0 J
Final: PE = 0 J, KE = 196.2 J
Total energy is conserved!
